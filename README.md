# Amazon_Sale_Report

Amazon Sales Report Dashboard built to analyze business performance across sales, orders, customer Behaviour, product categories, regions, fulfillment methods, courier status, and size-wise demand.

- The main goal of this project is :
   Which Product Categories has most to sales.
   Which regions has to generates the high revenue.
   To Preview the Customer Lifecycle Management over time period.
   Which Categories are in high demand.
   How orders are distributed across fulfillment and status types.
   How sales and orders change over time.

- Key Performance Indicators :
   Sales by Month : ~ 24.80 (Jun)
   Orders by Month : ~ 37,689 (Jun)
   Total Sales : ~ 83.29M
   Total Orders : ~ 128.9K
   Customer Count : ~ 120.20K

- Key Insights :
   T - Shirts (~49.4%) and Shrits (~27.9%) are the most top performing Categories by Sales, and Some blazzers (~14.0%) are the next.
   Maharastra (~ 14.6 M) and Karnataka (~ 11.4 M) are the highest revenue generating states followed by other sates in sales and orders.
   Amazon (~ 69.6%) Fulfillment method handles the majority of orders.
   Demand is strongest for M, L and XL sizes.
   Monthly sales shows slightly decline in the later months compared to the peak of April.

- Tools :
   Excel / CSV dataset for source data preparation.
   Python Pandas is used for data manipulation as handle the missing values, change the data types and fixing the Date format. Later follows EDA to preview the Sales trends and patterns over time, and checks the correlation    by Qty and Amount. Perform some statistics methods to preview the Avg of sales, data dispersin and max, min values in Dataset.
   Power BI for dashboard design and visualization.
   DAX / Measures for KPI calculations and summary metrics. It is used to check the Sales, Orders and Customers over the time.
